# What is CRM

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.  
It is a system used by companies to manage customer data, communication, sales, and support in one place.

# Why companies use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:
- Manage customers easily
- Track sales and leads
- Improve communication
- Automate business tasks
- Increase productivity

# Salesforce Objects

## Account
An Account represents a company or organization.

Example:
- TCS
- Infosys
- Amazon

## Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an Account.

Example:
- Employee
- Customer
- Manager

## Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential sales deal.

Example:
- Software purchase
- Service contract

# Real World Mapping

Account = Company  
Contact = Person in company  
Lead = Potential customer  
Opportunity = Deal or sale
