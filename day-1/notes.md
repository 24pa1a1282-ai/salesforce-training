# Day 1 Notes

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies manage customer data, communication, sales and support.

---

## What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage business workflows and customer relationships.

---

## What are Objects?
Objects are database tables in Salesforce.

Examples:
- Lead
- Contact
- Account

---

## What are Records?
Records are rows inside objects.

Example:
A single student inside Student object.
